# CHARACTER SHEET

## Name and Title
Thorin Ironbeard

## Age
155

## Family
Father: Gloin Ironbeard, deceased.
Mother: Indis Wildfire.
Grandfather: Dwalin Ironbeard.

## Background Story
Thorin was born in Erebor, the Dwarf Kingdom, and was the eldest son of Gloin Ironbeard. He was destined to become the leader of the Ironfoot clan and reclaim the Lonely Mountain from the Dragon Smaug. At the age of 18, Thorin led the Ironfoot warriors to Erebor and confronted Smaug, but he was forced to flee when the dragon attacked and killed his father.

## Personality and Character Traits
Thorin is a fierce, brave, and loyal Dwarf. He is known for his strength, courage, and determination. He is also a skilled warrior, hunter, and tracker. Thorin is fiercely protective of his clan and his homeland, and he will stop at nothing to see his goals accomplished.

## Occupation
Warrior, leader of the Ironfoot clan.

## Abilities and Skills
+ High strength and endurance.
+ Excellent at tracking and hunting.
+ Skilled with weapons and armor.
+ A master of the Dwarf language and lore.
+ Fearless in battle.

## Physical Appearance
Thorin has short, thick hair and a strong, muscular build. He wears a chainmail shirt, leather armor, and a Dwarven helm with a horned crest. He is known for his piercing, cold blue eyes and his distinctive beard.

## Clothing
Thorin always wears the colors of the Ironfoot clan: red and gold.

## Food Preferences
Thorin loves the taste of smoked meat, game, and fruit. He is not fond of bread and prefers ale over water.

## Favorite Quote
"The world is full of wonders, and I am a Dwarf. I seek adventure and danger. I will never grow weary." - Thorin Ironbeard
